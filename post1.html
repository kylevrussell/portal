Introduction

This blog is an exploration of a variety of persepctives on healthcare decisions. I’ll be drawing from my experiences in healthcare, technology, research, and my own journey as a patient living with heart disease. Along the way, I’ll explore topics such as decision-making, human cognition, system design, artificial intelligence, and the ways people and technologies shape one another over time. My hope is that these reflections will be useful to others who are interested in building tools, organizations, and systems that help people thrive.
